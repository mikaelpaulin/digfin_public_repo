Owner, Status and last-actor are read at the Scope × Step grain under Legal Entity = ~ (view AI_TWF_Scope); per-entity volume counts come from AI_TWF_LegalEntity; deadlines from AI_TWF_Deadlines. Sign-off completion is tracked via Status = "Done", not the numeric Completed count, which aggregates differently.
